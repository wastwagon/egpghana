Management of the platform — work covered each four months

This invoice covers ongoing management of the EGP website platform only. Domain renewals and hosting are billed separately. Below is the work delivered in each payment period.

Period 1 — April to July 2026 (due 30 April 2026)

  • Monitor site uptime, server health, and resolve incidents affecting public access
  • Apply security updates, patches, and dependency updates to the live platform
  • Support EGP staff with content updates (articles, publications, events, resources)
  • Maintain Debt Tracker, IMF Monitor, and Economic Dashboard data and display accuracy
  • Verify backups and data integrity; assist with institutional account transition planning
  • Minor fixes, performance tuning, and analytics review for the quarter

Period 2 — August to November 2026 (due 1 August 2026)

  • Continued platform monitoring, security hardening, and incident response
  • CMS and admin support for staff publishing and media uploads
  • Quarterly updates to economic indicators, dashboard figures, and chart data
  • AI chatbot and analytics upkeep; review visitor trends and report as needed
  • Progress hosting/domain ownership transfer to official EGP accounts where agreed
  • Bug fixes, link checks, and small enhancements agreed with EGP during the period

Period 3 — December 2026 to March 2027 (due 1 December 2026)

  • Ongoing management through year-end and into the next contract cycle
  • Security and backup verification before and after any hosting or DNS changes
  • Content and dashboard support for year-end publications and data releases
  • Admin user and access review; documentation for institutional handover
  • Prepare annual summary of platform management activities for EGP records
  • Advisory on renewal options for hosting, domains, and management for the following year
